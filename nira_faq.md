# Nira Data Custom Research Solutions - FAQ

## 1. Sampling & Respondent Recruitment

**What is Nira Data's sampling approach?**
Mobile-first, nationally representative sampling methodology based on quotas for age, gender, region and demographic variables. Recruitment occurs across mobile panels and digital channels.

**How do you ensure sample representativeness?**
The company applies demographic quotas during collection and applies weighting adjustments post-fieldwork to align with official population benchmarks like census data.

**What sampling frame do you use?**
Diverse sources including mobile apps, ad networks, social media, and opt-in research panels help reach underrepresented groups.

**What countries or markets do you cover?**
Nira Data covers growing global markets with nationally representative samples in developed markets and reliable urban/online samples elsewhere.

---

## 2. Data Quality & Fraud Prevention

**How does Nira Data ensure data quality?**
Multi-layered quality system includes device fingerprinting, response time analysis, duplicate detection, straight-lining checks, pattern detection, and geolocation validation.

**Do you block fraudulent or low-quality respondents?**
Yes—respondents failing quality checks are flagged in real time and removed before delivery.

**How do you detect bots?**
Using behavioral analytics, inconsistent answer detection, network-level checks (VPN/proxy detection), and CAPTCHA-style procedures.

**Do you clean the data before delivery?**
All datasets undergo automated cleaning plus human review.

---

## 3. Survey Design & Questionnaires

**What types of questions does Nira Data support?**
Currently limited to: single-choice and multiple-choice formats.

**Why limit surveys to multiple-choice questions?**
To ensure consistent performance across devices and operating systems and reduce respondent burden.

**Are there limitations on question and answer length?**
Questions must be under 100 characters (including spaces). Each answer option must be under 50 characters (including spaces).

**How many answer options can a question have?**
Typically no more than 6 answer options. Additional options require scrolling on mobile devices, which should be avoided.

**Can surveys include links?**
No, links are not allowed in survey questions or answer options.

**How many questions can I ask in a survey?**
The company recommends 15–20 questions maximum to avoid high dropout and lower-quality responses.

**Do you support multimedia (images, videos)?**
Image support varies by market; videos are restricted due to load-time variability across devices and networks.

---

## 4. Fieldwork & Response Collection

**How quickly can you collect responses?**
Standard samples (n=1,000 in major markets) typically complete within 24–48 hours; harder-to-recruit audiences may need longer.

**What time of day do respondents typically answer?**
Responses are collected throughout the day with peaks during commuting hours, evenings, and weekends.

**Do respondents get paid?**
No incentives are provided. This approach eliminates fraud and poor response behavior like speeding and inattentive responses.

---

## 5. Weighting & Adjustments

**Do you weight your data?**
Yes, when applicable. Weighting variables typically include age, gender, region, and sometimes education or ethnicity.

**What weighting method do you use?**
Industry-standard rim weighting (raking) to align sample distributions with official population benchmarks.

---

## 6. Privacy & Compliance

**Is your data collection GDPR-compliant?**
Yes—all activities adhere to GDPR and applicable data protection laws.

**Do you collect personally identifiable information (PII)?**
No, unless a project specifically requires it with explicit consent.

**How do you handle children's data?**
The company does not survey children under 16 and complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, and local regulations.

---

## 7. Technical & Device Considerations

**Are surveys mobile-only?**
Yes, surveys are optimized for smartphones, though tablets or desktops are theoretically possible.

**Do differences in devices affect data?**
Questions are designed to be device-agnostic with consistent rendering across iOS, Android, and varied screen sizes.

**What languages do you support?**
Most global languages, with translation services available or client-supplied options accepted.

**Can respondents skip questions?**
By default, most questions are mandatory unless response options like "None of the above" are included.

---

## 8. Deliverables & Outputs

**What data formats can you deliver?**
CSV, XLSX, JSON, and dashboards (when applicable).

---

## 9. Troubleshooting & Support

**What if I need help designing my survey?**
The team reviews, edits, or builds questionnaires to align with mobile-first best practices.

**What if incidence rate is low?**
Early notification provided; alternative targeting or quota modifications suggested.

**Can I track results over time?**
Yes—the platform supports continuous and repeated measurement for tracking research.
